Behaviorism

Behaviorism, also known as behavioral psychology, is a theory of learning based on the idea that all behaviors are acquired through conditioning. Conditioning occurs through interaction with the environment. Behaviorists believe that our responses to environmental stimuli shape our actions.

Skinner's Operant Conditioning

One of the features of our app that is related to Skinner’s theory is leaderboard this is considered as an feature in classify skinner stated behavior which is reinforced tends to be repeated behavior which is not reinforced tends to die out-or be extinguished. In classify app there is a game that depends on the topic of the user’s preference. The users that play will be put in the leaderboards and it is ranked from the highest to the lowest the highest gaining user will be put in to top and has a bigger amount of rewards that gets.

Pavlov's Classical Conditioning

Pavlov’s Classical Conditioning can be applied our app via the reward system we implemented, every time the player answers correctly points will be rewarded but if a player is on a hot streak or straight consecutive correct answers, the player will receive bonus points as well as the player will maintain or gains he/her standing on the leaderboard. The player will also receive notifications when he/she has not been playing for a long time.


Positive Reinforcement

Users having high scores on quizzes and being on a podium /leaderboard allows the users to purchase cosmetic items on their avatar as a reward and also completing tasks that gives reward points stimulates the users to use the app more frequently which is an effect of positive reinforcement.

Punishment

In our app we have included both positive and negative in the punishment. On the negative side we have provided a timer on each question if the player is taking too much of its time to answer. On the positive side we give points in every right answer that the player will have to add some motivation when they play again. The claimed rewards will be used in the games shop where they can buy items for their avatar.
