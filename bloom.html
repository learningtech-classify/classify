Bloom’s Taxonomy of Cognitive Development

Using Bloom’s Taxonomy, infused with technology, is an effective way to develop engaging learning activities on a continuum of complexity to improve teaching and learning. It can also be used as a tool to differentiate instruction in our classrooms to meet the needs of all students. In our application, we have a feature that applies the Bloom's Taxonomy theory. The said feature is called Multiplayer Q&A and Trivia feature which it engages the students to test their knowledge by answering different questions.
