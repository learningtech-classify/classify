Positive and Negative Reinforcement







Positive Reinforcement



In our application, we applied the feature of a reward system by giving points that can be used in the shop. The more points will be received from the multiplayer Q&A, the more points that can be used to buy items from the shop. It can create an effective way to motivate students by encouraging them through a system of rewards.




Negative Reinforcement

In our application, we applied the feature of a leaderboards in which the students are ranked accordingly in a podium. It strengthens the student’s behavior that avoids or removes a negative outcome.
