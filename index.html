About Classify


It is a game based learning platform where in it simulates a school environment as you discuss, share, and learn new things. The application can be used by the educational institutions, this will help in assessing the student's knowledge. It can also be used as an ice breaker. This might help the students to get motivated during class.


Who will benefit the app?

The people who will benefit from the application Classify are those in the educational institutions especially students and instructors.
.



How is your app different from existing applications similar to your application?

The application Classify is different from Kahoot because our application aims to provide multiplayer question and answer in a mobile-based environment. Unlike kahoot, Classify has more interactive features; this includes the shop feature wherein the user can buy cosmetics and items for his/her avatar in the shop through earned points.
.

The application is also similar to the Seesaw app which is more than just an assignment collector. Each button in this app has a different function, such as inserting pictures, capturing videos, and joining a class. Our app is different because it somehow connects with the users more using the cosmetics feature and also having a simpler UI design that can allow the students to easily use the app
